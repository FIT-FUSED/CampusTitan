## Vegetarian Meal Plan for 60g Protein:

- **Breakfast (30g Protein):**
   - Tofu Scramble: Crumble 1 block of extra firm tofu, season with turmeric, black salt, and pepper. Cook with chopped mushrooms, spinach, and onions. (Estimated Macros: 350 kcal, 30g Protein)
   - 1 slice of whole-grain toast with avocado spread. (100 kcal, 3g Protein)

- **Lunch (15g Protein):**
   - Veggie Burger: Try a black bean or lentil patty with lettuce, tomato, and a slice of cheese in a whole wheat bun. (450 kcal, 15g Protein)
   - Side Salad: Mix leafy greens with cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and a simple vinaigrette dressing. (150 kcal, 2g Protein)

- **Snack (5g Protein):**
   - Greek Yogurt with Berries: Choose a plain, unsweetened Greek yogurt and top with fresh blueberries and a drizzle of honey. (150 kcal, 5g Protein)

- **Dinner (10g Protein):**
   - Vegetable Stir-Fry: Sauté broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and baby corn with garlic and ginger. Serve with cooked quinoa. (350 kcal, 10g Protein)
   - Add a protein source like tempeh or seitan for extra protein. (Optional)

- **Evening Snack (optional, to reach protein goal):**
   - Protein Smoothie: Blend 1 scoop of vanilla vegetarian protein powder, 1 banana, almond milk, and peanut butter. (Adjust quantities for desired taste and protein content.)

**Tip:** You can also include protein-rich snacks like nuts, seeds, or vegetarian protein bars throughout the day
